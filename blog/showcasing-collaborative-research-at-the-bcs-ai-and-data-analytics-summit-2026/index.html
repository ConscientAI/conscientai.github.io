Showcasing collaborative research at the BCS AI & Data Analytics Summit 2026

May 29, 2026 · 2 min read

We were delighted to be part of the BCS AI & Data Analytics Summit 2026, organized by the Sri Lanka Section of BCS, The Chartered Institute for IT. Held in Colombo, the summit brought together policymakers, researchers, industry leaders and technology professionals to discuss the future of AI and data analytics in Sri Lanka, with a strong focus on collaboration, innovation and real‑world impact.

Our co‑founder, C.D. Athuraliya, delivered a talk titled Driving AI Innovation Through Collaborative Research, exploring how meaningful AI research can emerge from solving real business problems. Rather than treating research and commercial AI as separate efforts, the session highlighted how industry challenges can evolve into impactful research through continuous experimentation and close collaboration with academia and domain experts.

The talk shared our own journey—from building AI solutions for businesses to developing a research program that has resulted in multiple peer‑reviewed international publications, multilingual datasets and practical advances in areas such as opinion summarization and domain‑specific language models. More importantly, it emphasized that impactful AI innovation does not always require massive resources. With the right research culture, strong partnerships and a focus on real‑world problems, organizations can contribute new knowledge that benefits the wider AI community.

As AI adoption continues to accelerate, we believe the next step is not just using AI, but helping shape its future. Events like the BCS AI & Data Analytics Summit provide an important platform for sharing ideas, strengthening industry–academia collaboration and building an ecosystem where AI innovation can thrive.
