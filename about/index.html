ConscientAI is an award-winning AI technology company and research lab. We make AI accessible for businesses.

Our team brings together experience from world-leading institutions and enterprises, with years of hands-on work applying cutting-edge AI across domains. Since our inception in 2017, ConscientAI has grown into a globally recognized AI solutions provider, serving clients across North America, Europe, Australia, Japan, and Sri Lanka.

We combine deep research with practical engineering. Our work has been recognized through industry awards and publications at top-tier conferences, including research featured in National Geographic. ConscientAI operates globally and is incorporated in Sri Lanka (ConscientAI Labs (Pvt) Ltd) and the United States (ConscientAI LLC).

Why work with us?

Despite the hype around AI, building truly useful, production-ready AI products remains challenging. Doing it well requires a rare combination of top-tier talent, continuous research, and years of hands-on experience. Yet many businesses lack access to this level of AI expertise. As a result, teams often take the wrong approach, and many AI initiatives fail to deliver in real-world settings.

We bridge this gap by bringing together applied experience, deep technical expertise, and ongoing AI research to deliver solutions that go beyond prototypes. We work as an extension of our clients’ teams, providing both strategic guidance and hands-on engineering.

This enables our clients to build scalable, maintainable AI systems aligned with their long-term business goals. We focus on turning AI from a buzzword into a reliable, high-impact business capability that solves real problems and delivers measurable value.

Who we work with

We work with technology companies, startups, and SMEs that are either exploring AI to solve business and operational challenges or building AI-powered products and services.

We typically work with founders, CXOs, or Heads of Data/AI who are responsible for driving technology strategy and execution. Our services cover the full AI lifecycle—from strategy and product engineering to deployment and ongoing improvement.
